AI Design Review FAQ

What is an AI design review?

An AI design review is an automated analysis of your UI design using artificial intelligence. Design Snapper's AI design review engine evaluates your screens against WCAG standards, usability heuristics, and visual design principles — delivering objective, actionable feedback instantly.

How accurate is Design Snapper's AI design review?

Design Snapper's AI design reviewer uses Vision AI to analyze your UI at the pixel level. It reliably catches WCAG contrast failures, touch target issues, ARIA state problems, and layout inconsistencies with high precision, matching or exceeding what a human reviewer would catch in a manual review.

Can AI replace a human design reviewer?

For rules-based checks (WCAG, spacing, accessibility), AI design review is faster, more consistent, and more thorough than a human reviewer.
